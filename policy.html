GANT Smallholder Protection & Fair Market Policy

GANT was built on a simple but powerful idea: that every farmer, no matter how small, should have a fair shot at selling their produce for a transparent, real price — not one dictated by middlemen, local cartels, or speculative traders gaming global systems for profit.

We pledge to act not as a neutral clearinghouse, but as a market governor and steward of smallholder prosperity. Through algorithm design, transparent pricing, and real-world governance, we will protect fairness at scale.

Algorithmic Smallholder Preference

Smallholders and co-ops will receive built-in visibility boosts to prevent industrial suppliers from crowding them out...

Progressive Pricing & Tiered Fees

Base fees are 3–4%, but progressive tiers activate above $100B GMV, ensuring large players reinvest into smallholder resilience...

Position Limits & Market Share Caps

No buyer can hold more than 10% corridor market share. Smart contracts enforce caps automatically...

Phantom Order Prevention

Every bid is backed by locked funds or verified capacity. Fake liquidity and repeated trade failures are blocked...

Institutional Buyer Sourcing Rules

Large buyers must source a portion of supply from smallholders/co-ops once corridors exceed $500M GMV...

Fair Trade Index

A quarterly ranking of buyers/sellers based on fairness and compliance with GANT principles...

Market Integrity Board Oversight

Independent representation from farmers, Indigenous groups, and governments ensures trust and enforcement...

Data Transparency & Access

All verified users, especially smallholders, get free real-time access to regional bid/ask spreads and dashboards...

ESG Transition Fund Logic

1% from non-ESG trades is ring-fenced to support farmers in transitioning toward sustainable practices...

In conclusion: Every corridor, every expansion, every trade is measured against fairness for smallholders. This is our covenant: efficiency will never come at the cost of equity.
