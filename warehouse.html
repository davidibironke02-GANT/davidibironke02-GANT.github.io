Warehouse & Operations

The Physical Backbone of the GANT Exchange

Our operations center in Northeast Calgary is the anchor of the entire GANT Exchange model. It is designed to convert farmers’ bulk grain into export-ready, identity-preserved lots that global buyers can trust. Every pallet processed in this facility becomes the basis for instant settlement, financing, risk scoring, and IoT-enabled traceability.

Why a Warehouse-Centric Model?

Traditional agritrade platforms list commodities they never physically touch. GANT operates differently. By managing a real facility, we take responsibility for the physical transformation of grain and generate verified data that buyers and regulators can rely on. This ensures quality, compliance, and traceability start from the very first moment the grain arrives.

What Happens Inside the Facility

Our center integrates processing, quality control, storage, and documentation under one workflow. This efficiency enables a consistent and trustworthy supply chain from farmer to global buyer.

  • Advanced drying and cleaning lines designed for Western Canadian grain, ensuring moisture and purity meet export standards.
  • Quality testing laboratory equipped with NIR analysis, protein measurement, moisture checks, and mycotoxin screening.
  • Identity-preserved packaging into 25 kg bags, automatically palletized into verified 1 MT units.
  • Warehouse Management System that tracks each pallet with a unique BatchLotID and Serial number.
  • IoT-enabled environment monitoring capturing temperature and humidity throughout storage, with data stored securely in cloud systems.
  • Phytosanitary and export documentation flow ready for CFIA certification and international clearance.

How the Warehouse Supports the Exchange

Every process inside the warehouse becomes structured data on the platform. When a buyer evaluates a lot, they see real quality metrics, storage conditions, operator-of-record details, and corridor-level risk insights. This reduces uncertainty, enables instant settlement after trade approval, and allows buyers to connect financing directly to verified physical goods.
