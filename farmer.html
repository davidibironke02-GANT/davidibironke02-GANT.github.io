Farmers & Cooperatives

Transform bulk grain into warehouse-verified, identity-preserved lots. Receive instant settlement once trades are approved by the Corridor Risk Engine — all while staying completely anonymous to buyers through your Farmer Unique ID (FUID).

Why Farmers Use GANT

Pricing Freedom

Set your own price using transparent market benchmarks and corridor conditions. No hidden discounts or forced downgrades.

Instant Settlement

Payment is released immediately after your trade is approved by the Corridor Risk Engine. No delays and no negotiation risks.

Verified Processing

Your grain is dried, cleaned, quality-tested, bagged into 25-kilogram units, palletized, serialized, and stored under continuous IoT monitoring.

Anonymous Global Access

Buyers never see your identity. They only see your system-generated Farmer Unique ID (FUID), ensuring fairness across the market.

How the Process Works

Step 1 — Deliver Grain to the Warehouse

Farmers deliver bulk grain to our processing facility. Grain is offloaded, moisture-tested, and placed in the processing queue.

Step 2 — Processing & Quality Verification

Grain is dried, cleaned, cooled, and fully tested. Each 25-kilogram bag receives a BatchLotID and Serial Number for traceability.

Step 3 — Review Your Verified Value Index

You receive a private Verified Value Index based on quality, sustainability, processing outcomes, and corridor risk data. Learn more about the Verified Value Index →

Step 4 — List Your Grain on the Exchange

You set your selling price and list your lot directly to global buyers. Your FUID ensures anonymity throughout the process.

Step 5 — Trade Approval & Instant Settlement

When a buyer submits a bid, the Corridor Risk Engine evaluates route conditions and compliance. If the trade is approved, payment is released instantly.

Storage, Fees & Privacy

14 Days Free Storage

Every pallet receives 14 days of free storage. Storage charges only begin after this period and are deducted after your sale.

Transparent Fee Structure

Processing, handling, warehousing, and platform fees are displayed clearly in your dashboard. All charges are applied only after a successful transaction.

Guaranteed Privacy

Your personal identity is never revealed to buyers. Only your Farmer Unique ID (FUID) appears in the marketplace.
