How the Process Works

Step 1 — Deliver Grain to the Warehouse

Farmers deliver bulk grain to the Calgary warehouse. Grain is offloaded, moisture-tested, and assigned a temporary holding bin for processing.

Step 2 — Processing & Quality Verification

Your grain is dried, cleaned, cooled, and tested in the on-site quality laboratory. Each 25-kilogram bag receives a BatchLotID and Serial Number for traceability.

Step 3 — Review Your Verified Value Index

Once processing is complete, you receive a private Verified Value Index (VVI) score based on quality, processing outcomes, sustainability, and corridor risk factors. Learn more about the Verified Value Index →

Step 4 — List Your Grain on the Exchange

After reviewing your VVI score, you set your desired price and make your lot available to global buyers on the GANT Exchange.

Step 5 — Trade Approval & Instant Settlement

When a buyer places a bid, the Corridor Risk Engine evaluates the transaction. If approved, settlement occurs immediately and your funds are released without delay.

Storage, Fees & Privacy

14 Days Free Storage

Every pallet receives 14 days of free storage after processing. Storage fees apply thereafter, deducted after sale.

Transparent Fees

Processing, handling, logistics coordination, and platform fees are clearly displayed in your dashboard and deducted only after a successful sale.

Full Privacy

Buyers do not see your name or location. They only see your system-generated Farmer Unique ID (FUID), ensuring an unbiased marketplace.
