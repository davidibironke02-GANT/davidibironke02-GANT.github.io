How GANT Works

The global agritrade sector grapples with systemic inefficiencies: fragmented supply chains, opaque pricing, protracted settlement cycles, and pervasive risks from fraud to volatility. These inefficiencies erode trust, inflate costs, and marginalize small-scale producers. GANT is engineered to solve these challenges.

Our blockchain-enabled marketplace orchestrates end-to-end transactions with precision and security. Producers list commodities, buyers bid above dynamic floor prices, and funds are escrowed instantly through smart contracts. Our AI-powered Corridor Risk Engine evaluates risks in real time, ensuring trades remain secure and resilient.

Once matched, goods move to Collection Centers for quality verification, and producers are paid in a T+1 settlement cycle. Buyers receive complete digital documentation and IoT-powered shipment tracking, creating a transparent and trust-driven ecosystem.

Through this model, GANT embeds intelligence, security, and fairness into every transaction—paving the way for a sustainable and inclusive future in agriculture.
