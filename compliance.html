Compliance and Documentation

A structured documentation system that removes uncertainty and ensures every shipment complies with destination-country requirements.

A predictable documentation framework

GANT provides buyers and regulators with a complete, verified documentation pack for every trade. All records originate from our controlled warehouse environment to ensure accuracy and consistency.

Documentation generated from verified warehouse data

Every lot listed on GANT comes from processed and quality tested grain inside our processing facility. All documentation is produced from structured, verified data rather than self-reported information, which removes uncertainty for buyers and auditors.

Export-ready documents included with every trade

The platform prepares all required export documents automatically, including commercial invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, quality certificate, compliance summary and phytosanitary documentation when required.

Phytosanitary certification with CFIA

The Canadian Food Inspection Agency certifies grain that leaves our warehouse, ensuring alignment with destination sanitary requirements.

Visit Canadian Food Inspection Agency →

QR linked documentation for buyers

After a trade, buyers receive a single QR code for all pallets in their order. Scanning the QR provides instant access to quality test summaries, farmer sustainability practices, compliance documents and post delivery shipment condition summaries.

Five year digital record retention

All shipment documentation is stored securely for five years. Buyers and regulators can retrieve records at any time without requesting files from sellers.

Government and institutional visibility

Approved government partners and institutional buyers can access structured documentation records for shipments moving through their corridors, supporting oversight and food safety processes.
