Why Buyers Work with GANT

Our role is simple: we make it easier to buy Canadian grain with confidence. You keep full control of your logistics and destination handling; we focus on quality, financing, documentation and risk visibility along the corridor.

Consistent, Verified Quality

Every lot is processed through a modern grain facility with drying, cleaning and a dedicated quality testing lab.

Export-Ready Documentation

We prepare the core export documents: invoice, packing list, certificate of origin, phytosanitary certificate and chain-of-custody records.

Ex Works Pickup

You handle trucking, rail and ocean carriers. You pay only for grain and your own logistics, duties and port costs.

Risk Assessment

All bids are escrow-backed and must pass corridor-level risk checks before approval.

How Buying Works

You see the information you need, place a real bid, let the risk engine do its work, then pick up when the cargo is ready.

Step 1 — Browse Available Lots

Lots include quality tests, warehouse history, sustainability indicators and a Verified Value Index score. Learn more →

Step 2 — Place 20% Escrow Deposit

You deposit 20% of trade value to prevent phantom orders. Funds are held by fintech partners.

Step 3 — Corridor Risk Engine

We assess tariffs, geopolitical events, disruptions and environmental conditions before approval.

Step 4 — Documentation and Release

Once approved, we prepare your export documentation and schedule warehouse release.

Step 5 — EXW Pickup

You pick up from our processing facility. Containers include a QR code and a single use IoT monitor.

Step 6 — Delivery and QR Scan

Scanning the QR unlocks quality history, storage records and the container’s journey.
